Recommendations
Prioritized actions based on data analysis
1
Activate Smart Disputes on Joist + Invoice Simple
Win rates of 3-5% on Joist and Invoice Simple represent $3.7M in disputed dollars with virtually no defense. Even a modest win rate improvement (3% → 15%) on this base would recover $400-500K annually. Amendment in signature. Once signed, opt-out rollout to all CAs on Joist and Invoice Simple first.
Immediate — This Quarter
2
Investigate Joist "Debit Not Authorized" Large-Dollar Fraud
13 disputes averaging $28K each ($366K total losses). These are likely large-dollar single-transaction fraud from synthetic identity / business impersonation cases. Pull the specific CAs generating these disputes and cross-reference against Chris's fraudops submissions.
Immediate — This Quarter
3
Evidence Submission Audit
The 3-5% win rate on Joist/Invoice Simple could indicate CAs aren't submitting evidence at all. DrChrono's 22% win rate proves evidence submission works when it happens. Smart Disputes solves this automatically by handling evidence submission for CAs.
Immediate — This Quarter
4
Identity Verification for High-Risk Onboarding
The card cashing pattern on Joist (fake businesses, AI-generated websites, debit-only payouts) could be caught at onboarding with Identity verification. Currently investigating availability for SMR/SNBL platforms.
Medium-Term — Next Quarter
5
Financial Connections for Bank Verification
The "debit-only payouts" fraud pattern could be disrupted by verifying bank account ownership before enabling payouts. Already active ($10.6K ONR). Evaluate expanding to mandatory verification for new Joist CAs.
Medium-Term — Next Quarter
6
Radar for Platforms (RfP)
Platform-level fraud rules could catch patterns across CAs (same fingerprints, same fraud vectors) before disputes occur. NEW 2026 product. Requires Reject API gate expansion for SMR accounts. Engineering scoping in progress.
Medium-Term — Next Quarter
